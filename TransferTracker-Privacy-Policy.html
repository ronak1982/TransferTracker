Privacy Policy for TransferTracker

Last updated: January 7, 2026

Introduction

TransferTracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

TransferTracker collects and stores the following information:

  • Transfer Lists: Product transfer information you create, including product names, quantities, costs, and notes.
  • User Data: Names and identifiers you provide for authorized users of your transfer lists.
  • iCloud Data: Your data is synced through Apple's iCloud service using CloudKit.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (creating and managing transfer lists)
  • Sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
  • Enable sharing of transfer lists with authorized users

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in two locations:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored locally on your device for offline access.
  • iCloud (CloudKit): Data is automatically synced to your private iCloud account for backup and cross-device access.

We do not have access to your data. All information is stored in your personal iCloud account, which is protected by Apple's security measures and your iCloud credentials.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Data sharing only occurs when:

  • You explicitly share a transfer list with another user via CloudKit sharing
  • Shared lists are only accessible to users you specifically authorize

Third-Party Services

TransferTracker uses Apple CloudKit for data synchronization. Your use of CloudKit is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Data Retention

Your data is retained in your iCloud account until you:

  • Delete it from within the app
  • Delete the app and clear your iCloud data
  • Disable iCloud for TransferTracker in your device settings

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your data by removing transfer lists or deleting the app
  • Control who has access to your shared transfer lists
  • Disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

TransferTracker is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
